Hobbies

Indian Classical Dance

I developed a love for dancing at a young age when my parents enrolled me in a dance school to learn Bharatnatyam, an Indian classical dance form, at the age of 5. Performing on stage was always a joy. Despite living abroad, I continue to stay connected to my Indian roots by participating in cultural programs to showcase and share my heritage.

Weight Training

Going to the gym has become a significant part of my routine, not just for physical health but also for mental well-being. It's amazing how a good workout can uplift your mood and reduce stress. I've learned a lot about fitness and nutrition along the way, and I'm always looking for ways to improve my workouts and diet to reach my goals.

Travelling

I have a passion for traveling and exploring the open road, especially in the USA. I absolutely love the freedom and adventure that comes with driving. My most recent adventure took me to Yellowstone National Park, where I was captivated by the stunning landscapes and natural beauty.
