Bio

I am currently a graduate research assistant at the University of Nebraska Lincoln. My main research focus is perception-based safety monitoring and verification of autonomous robots at construction sites. I obtained my bachelor's degree from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), New Delhi.
